My Early Adventures

Life is a journey filled with moments that shape who we become. My early adventures began with curiosity, exploration, and learning. From childhood experiences to my formative years in school, each stage contributed to my growth and aspirations. Exploring Interests & Hobbies As a child, I was always drawn to problem-solving, and creativity. Whether it was tinkering with gadgets, playing strategic games, or exploring new places, I enjoyed discovering how things worked. My love for football and basketball also played a big role in teaching me teamwork, discipline, and perseverance. School Life & Learning Experiences At Joy Blessed Stars Primary School, I built a strong academic foundation, developing a keen interest in subjects like math and science. Moving on to Kiereini Boys High School, I not only expanded my knowledge but also participated in activities that helped shape my personality. One of my memorable moments was competing in a math contest, which challenged my analytical thinking and problem-solving skills. First Steps into IT My fascination with technology grew as I got my first hands-on experience with computers, coding, and software development. This led me to pursue my studies in Information Technology at Zetech University, where I developed my technical skills and worked on impactful projects. These early adventures shaped my ambitions, strengthened my skills, and fueled my passion for innovation. They continue to inspire me as I navigate my journey toward becoming a software developer and entrepreneur

Memorable Experience Life is filled with defining moments that leave a lasting impact. One of my most memorable experiences was developing a sales platform for men's grooming products during my diploma studies at Zetech University. This project was a turning point in my journey as an aspiring software developer. The Challenge & Learning Process At first, the project seemed daunting, as it required coding, database integration, and user interface design. With the guidance of my mentors, Madam Hellen and Mr. Boniface, I navigated through challenges, learning how to troubleshoot errors, optimize the database, and ensure a seamless user experience. The Achievement Completing the project successfully gave me a sense of accomplishment and confidence in my skills. It reinforced my passion for software development and inspired me to pursue a future in building innovative digital solutions. This experience taught me valuable lessons about problem-solving, perseverance, and the power of learning through hands-on projects—an unforgettable milestone in my academic and professional journey.

The Day I Discovered...

There are moments in life that shape our future, and for me, that moment came the day I wrote my first piece of code: "Hello, World!". It was a simple line of text, but seeing it displayed on the screen sparked a passion that would define my career path. The First Step into Coding My journey into programming began with curiosity. I had always been fascinated by computers, software, and how digital systems worked. The day I typed print("Hello, World!") and saw the output appear, I realized the power of coding—how a few lines of instructions could create something meaningful. The Start of a Lifelong Passion That moment ignited my interest in software development and motivated me to pursue formal education in Information Technology. I started with a Certificate in IT, where I learned the basics of computing, networking, and programming. This foundation led me to pursue a Diploma in IT at Zetech University, where I deepened my skills and developed a sales platform for men's grooming products as a project. Now, as I continue my academic journey by pursuing a Bachelor’s in Information Technology, my passion for coding and problem-solving grows even stronger. A Future Built on Innovation Looking back, that simple “Hello, World!” was more than just a beginner’s program—it was the first step toward my dream of building software solutions that impact lives. As I advance in my studies and work toward starting my own coding company, I remain committed to learning, innovating, and pushing the boundaries of technology.
